Welcome to LiquidLens!

About Us & Our Approach

We are a group of enthusiastic students working together on this water pollution monitoring project called LiquidLens. Our aim is to make water testing simple, smart, and accessible for everyone.

Our team is divided into different roles ‚Äî one handles the hardware and sensors, another takes care of the software and web app, and others help with data analysis and project design. Everyone brings something unique to the table.

Our approach is to use affordable sensors with an ESP32 board to collect data like pH, TDS, turbidity, and temperature from water samples. This data is sent to our web app, where users can see it clearly and get a report about the water's quality.

We believe this project can help people, schools, and even local authorities check water health easily and take action if needed. LiquidLens is our small step towards a cleaner and healthier environment.
